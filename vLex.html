vLex Knowledge Base

vLex is a Spanish company that provides a search engine that indexes legal documents. It is popular with law schools in Spanish and English speaking countries.

In 2021, I supported the renovation of their Knowledge Base website.

This project consisted of starting the knowledge base for the company to share with users. The project started from an old and outdated manual. The main change proposed in this project was to change it from feature-based to a more user-friendly approach. This is reflected in the "Getting started" and the "How-to guides" sections.
